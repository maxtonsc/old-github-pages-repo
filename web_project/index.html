About GenieOS

GenieOS is a simple to use, affordable, cloud based data analytics platform focused on small to medium enterprise. Our goal is to make the platform so intuitive that GenieOS can be used in-house by any business without requiring specialist infrastructure or HR. Utilising Semantic tools to extract and infer meaning from data, meeting business’ needs to harness real value from complexity; making technology work for business.

Ultimately everyone will be able to build their own solution and extract value from their data assets.  

Problem

There is a ‘data gap’ where organisations have the data but lack an easy to use independent decision environment designed for non-technical users. Currently there is significant cost and effort to gain insight from your data. No one in the market is developing a solution that can affordably allow SME’s to analyse and gain insight from their information.

IDC predicts that worldwide revenues just for big data and analytics will grow to more than $203B by 2020.

Solution

We are entering a new era of exponentially improved interactions between technology and business via semantic and machine learning tools.
